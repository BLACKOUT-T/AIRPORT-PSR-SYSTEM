Airport Primary Surveillance Radar (PSR)

The PSR is a ground-based radiolocation system used within terminal areas. As a non-cooperative sensor, it detects aircraft independently by processing microwave "echoes" that bounce off the surface.

Purpose and Safety

  • Safety & Separation: PSR calculates range and bearing to maintain safe aircraft separation.
  • Weather Detection: Tracks precipitation levels to help pilots navigate around hazards.
  • Digital Display: Data is relayed to STARS consoles in air traffic towers.
