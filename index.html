What is RADAR?

Radar is a system typically consisting of a synchronized radio transmitter and receiver that emits radio waves to detect aircraft by processing reflected electromagnetic energy.

Airport PSR

Primary Surveillance Radar (PSR) acts as a non-cooperative sensor. It detects reflective objects independently by processing "echoes" of microwave pulses that bounce off the aircraft's surface.

Purpose

The PSR calculates range and bearing to maintain safe separation and triggers ground-based conflict alerts while tracking precipitation levels.
