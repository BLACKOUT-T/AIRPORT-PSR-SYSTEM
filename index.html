What is RADAR?

Radar is a system typically consisting of a synchronized radio transmitter and receiver that emits radio waves to detect objects.

Airport PSR

The Primary Surveillance Radar (PSR) determines aircraft positions by utilizing reflected electromagnetic energy. It acts as a non-cooperative sensor, processing microwave "echoes" that bounce off the aircraft's surface.
